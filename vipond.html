The Vipond Group Has Launched a New Residential Real Estate Division!

Message from Bill Vipond: I am writing to share with you that I (aka: The Vipond Group) have opened a new division in the company, which will be exclusively devoted to residential real estate sales. Our commercial division is thriving, but over the years, I have had many clients ask me to assist them with the sale of their homes, and/or the purchase of a new one.

In an effort to serve our clients, and their families, friends, and colleagues, who are in need of residential real estate services, I have teamed up with Jim Clark. Jim and I have been working together for many years, and like me, he has vast experience and knowledge in both commercial and residential real estate.

We’ll provide these real estate brokerage services to you as the “Vipond | Clark Home Team”.

Our goal is to become a very valuable resource for you, your families, friends, and colleagues, who might be in need of residential real estate services.
